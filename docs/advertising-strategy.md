## Tier 1 — Free / Organic (Start This Week, Zero Ad Spend)

### 1. TikTok Organic Content — HIGHEST ROI, zero cost

This is the single best channel for spiritual content right now. Tarot TikTok is a massive, engaged community, and the algorithm rewards creators from zero followers if the content resonates. The visual nature of tarot cards + the steampunk aesthetic of your Astral Threads deck is inherently shareable.

**Content angles that work in this niche:**
- **"Pick a card" interactive readings** — show 3 card-backs, tell viewers to pick one, reveal the meaning. These get massive saves and shares. Use your card-back image.
- **"What your zodiac sign needs to hear today"** — 12-part carousel or rapid-fire video. Each gets screenshotted and shared.
- **Card-of-the-day pulls** — literally film yourself drawing a card and reading its meaning. 15-30 seconds. Daily consistency builds followers fast.
- **The Decan Engine explainer** — "Did you know every tarot card sits at an exact 10° celestial degree? Here's what that means…" — this is genuinely novel and educational content that sets you apart from every generic tarot account.
- **Behind-the-scenes of the card art** — show the steampunk mech creatures, the crystal orb devices, the design process. People love art process content and it builds brand mystique.
- **"Yes or No" quick readings** — point people to tarotbyte.app/readings/yes-no for their own free reading.

**Posting cadence:** 3-5x per week. Use trending audio where natural. Always end with a CTA to tarotbyte.app for a free reading. The bio link is your conversion mechanism.

**Why this ranks #1:** The tarot/spiritual TikTok community is large, engaged, and underserved by *quality* brands (most content is amateur). Your professional art + unique system = instant credibility. Zero ad spend. Compounds over time.

### 2. Pinterest Organic + Pins — Second highest ROI, long-tail compounding

Pinterest is **the** platform for spiritual/wellness content discovery. It functions as a visual search engine, not a social feed — meaning pins keep driving traffic for months or years after posting, unlike TikTok/Instagram where content dies in 72 hours. The spiritual audience on Pinterest is massive and actively searching.

**Pin types to create:**
- **Tarot spread infographics** — "Past Present Future Spread: What It Reveals" with your card art. Link to the reading page.
- **Zodiac sign guides** — "Aries Energy: What It Means in Tarot" featuring your Aries mech art. Link to /oracle.
- **Card meaning cards** — beautiful graphics with a single card's meaning. These get saved and re-pinned for years.
- **Decan calendar graphics** — "The 36 Decans: Which One Are You In?" — this is unique IP that no one else can replicate.
- **Aesthetic mood boards** — steampunk tarot, celestial decor, cosmic vibes. Broad reach, brand association.

**Why this ranks #2:** Pinterest users are in **planning and discovery mode** — they're looking for things to try, buy, and save. This converts to site visits better than any other social platform for visual/lifestyle products. 70% of spiritual products are bought online; Pinterest feeds that pipeline. And pins are evergreen — a pin posted today can still drive traffic in 2027.

This could be your highest-traffic organic page within 3-6 months.

## Tier 2 — Low-Cost Paid (Start After Organic Baseline, ~$5-15/day)

### 4. Pinterest Ads — Best paid channel for this niche

Pinterest ads in the wellness/spirituality space have strong CPMs (typically $3-8 per thousand impressions) and the audience is already in a discovery mindset. Unlike Facebook/Meta, Pinterest users actively *want* to find new products to try.

**Campaign structure:**
- **Objective:** Consideration → Traffic (send people to tarotbyte.app/readings)
- **Targeting:** Interests: "Spirituality," "Astrology," "Tarot," "Crystals," "Mysticism," "Wellness." Also layer in "New Age" and "Self-Care."
- **Creative:** Your card art is your best asset. Use the steampunk mech zodiac images and decan card art as pin creatives. "Get a free tarot reading with a celestial twist →" as the overlay text.
- **Budget:** Start at $5-10/day. Pinterest rewards good creative with lower CPMs.
- **Key advantage:** Pins that perform well as ads often continue performing organically after the campaign ends, compounding your investment.

### 5. Meta (Facebook/Instagram) Ads — High reach, test carefully

Meta has the largest audience and best targeting granularity, but the spiritual/tarot niche has historically faced ad policy friction (Meta sometimes flags "psychic services" as restricted). **This is manageable but requires careful creative.**

**What works:**
- Frame everything as "entertainment" and "self-reflection" — never "fortune telling" or "predictions." Your Terms of Service already positions this correctly.
- Use "mindfulness," "self-discovery," "reflection," "astrology" as framing — these pass policy review easily.
- **Targeting:** Interests in astrology, tarot, mysticism, wellness, self-care, mindfulness, yoga, crystals. Ages 18-45. The Gen Z/Millennial overlap is your sweet spot.
- **Creative:** Video ads showing a card flip animation → "What does your energy say today? Get a free reading →" Link to /readings/yes-no (your lowest-friction free entry point).
- **Budget:** Start at $10-15/day, test 3-4 creatives, kill the losers, scale the winners.
- **Warning:** Monitor for policy flags. If an ad gets rejected, appeal it and adjust language. Keep "for entertainment purposes only" visible.

## Tier 3 — Partnership & Community (Medium Effort, High Trust)

### 6. Tarot/Astrology Creator Collaborations

Reach out to tarot and astrology creators on TikTok and Instagram (1K-50K followers — micro-influencers are affordable and have high trust). Offer them a free Decan Engine reading in exchange for an honest review or mention. Your unique system is a natural talking point — "I tried this tarot site that maps every card to an exact zodiac degree" is genuinely interesting content for them.

**Why micro-influencers over big ones:** A 5K-follower tarot creator has a highly engaged, trusting audience. A single mention can drive 50-200 highly-qualified visitors. At $0-50 cost (a free reading + maybe a small fee), the ROI per acquisition is exceptional. Big influencers charge $500-5000 for a post with lower trust per follower.

### 7. Reddit & Spiritual Communities (Free, High Trust, Be Authentic)

r/tarot (450K+ members), r/astrology (2M+ members), r/spirituality, and r/witchcraft are massive communities. **Do not spam.** Do:
- Answer questions genuinely and helpfully. When someone asks "what does the Two of Wands mean?" give a thoughtful answer and *naturally* mention that your site has a free reading that includes the decan layer.
- Share your card art in communities that allow it — the steampunk mech aesthetic will generate genuine interest.
- Do an AMA-style post: "I built a tarot site that maps every card to an exact 10° zodiac degree. Ask me anything about the system." Authentic founder stories do well on Reddit.
- **Critical:** Read each subreddit's rules on self-promotion. Many allow it in specific threads or with specific framing. Breaking rules gets you banned and burns the channel.

### 8. The Weekly Byte Newsletter (Your Owned Channel)

You already have the signup mechanism on the homepage. This is your most important long-term asset — an owned audience you don't have to pay to reach.

**Content for the newsletter:**
- Weekly card-of-the-week with a mini-reading
- "The cosmic energy this week" — tie it to current planetary positions
- A decan spotlight — feature one of your 36 decans with its title and meaning
- Gentle CTA to get a full reading on the site

**Why this matters:** Every social platform can change its algorithm or ban you. Your email list is yours. A list of 1,000 engaged subscribers is worth more than 10,000 random social followers because you can reach them for free, forever, and email converts 3-5x better than social for this audience.

## What NOT to Do

- **Don't run Google Search ads yet** — "tarot reading" keywords are expensive ($2-5/click) and dominated by big players (Keen, California Psychics, Kasamba). Your budget is better spent on visual/social discovery channels where you can stand out. Reconsider search ads only after you have brand recognition.
- **Don't spend on big influencers** — $500+ per post with low trust ROI. Stick to micro-influencers and organic.
- **Don't use "fortune telling" or "psychic" language in ads** — triggers platform policy restrictions and attracts the wrong audience. Stick to "self-reflection," "astrology," "entertainment."
- **Don't spread too thin** — pick TikTok + Pinterest as your two core channels first. Add more only after these are running consistently.
